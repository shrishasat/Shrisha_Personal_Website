Hello world!


I’m a Computational Neuroscience researcher with a background in Machine Learning and Biomedical Engineering. My work sits at the exciting intersection of brains, code, and curiosity — from building medical devices to modelling how the brain thinks, learns, and speaks.


Right now, I’m especially focused on decoding brain activity from language and vice versa using Large Language Models (LLMs) and brain data.


Outside of research, I’m deeply curious about bioastronautics (yes, team space), and when I’m not thinking about neurons or neural networks, you’ll probably find me passionately debating cricket.



Thanks for stopping by — this space shares my work, ideas, and experiments exploring the brain and beyond.

Training models, decoding brains, and occasionally confusing both.
